**Article 39.3 – Grid‑place penalty**

> “If any of the four penalties above are imposed … the stewards may impose a **grid‑place penalty on the driver at their next Race**.”  

*2026 wording*: “…the stewards may impose a **grid‑place penalty on the driver at their next Feature Race**.”  

**Interpretation (no tyre‑related issue)** – When a driver occupies a grid position that he/she has not qualified for, the stewards may apply the above grid‑place penalty, which is carried out at the driver’s next race (2025) or next Feature Race (2026).  

*Reference*: Article 39.3 of the Formula 3 Sporting Regulations (2025 / 2026).
