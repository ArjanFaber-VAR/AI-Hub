**During the race** – 10 seconds stop‑and‑go (mandatory)   
*Reference: 2026 Formula 3 Sporting Regulations Art. 24.15 (“Failing to use wet‑weather tyres if the Race Director has so directed”) – penalty applied on‑track.*

**After the race** – Grid‑place penalty applied at the driver’s next Feature Race   
*Reference: 2026 Penalty Guidelines (“If any of the four penalties above are imposed … the stewards may impose a grid‑place penalty on the driver at their next Feature Race”).*
